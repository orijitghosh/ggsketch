# Articles

### All vignettes

- [Controlling the
  sketch](https://orijitghosh.github.io/ggsketch/articles/customizing.md):

  The shared sketch parameters — roughness, bowing, n_passes, and seed —
  and how each changes the hand-drawn look.

- [Fill styles in
  depth](https://orijitghosh.github.io/ggsketch/articles/fill-styles.md):

  The ten stroked fill styles (including scribble, stipple, and pencil
  shading), the separate watercolour wash, how the hachure scan-line
  filler works, and how to tune angle, gap, and weight.

- [Gallery — every
  geom](https://orijitghosh.github.io/ggsketch/articles/gallery.md):

  A tour of every ggsketch geom with runnable examples, from bars and
  lines to histograms, violins, intervals, Q-Q plots, quantile fits,
  reference lines, curves, and annotations — plus the 2.0 additions:
  dumbbell, slope, beeswarm, ridgeline, streamgraph, waffle, treemap and
  calendar charts, hand-drawn networks and sf maps, drawing media,
  watercolour fills, textured paper, and hand-drawn coordinate frames.

  theme_sketch() light and dark presets, textured paper grounds, the
